Crop Timeline
  • Step 1: Land Preparation

    Prepare the land using ploughs or tractors. Remove weeds and level the field.

  • Step 2: Sowing

    Sow seeds using recommended spacing and depth based on crop variety.

  • Step 3: Fertilization

    Apply fertilizers (NPK) as per the soil test and crop requirement.

  • Step 4: Irrigation

    Irrigate at the right stages like germination, flowering, and grain filling.

  • Step 5: Harvesting

    Harvest when the crop is mature — check color change, grain hardening, etc.
